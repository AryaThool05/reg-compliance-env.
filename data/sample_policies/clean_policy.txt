PRIVACY POLICY — CloudSync Solutions Ltd.

Effective Date: 1 January 2026
Data Protection Officer: privacy@cloudsync-solutions.eu

1. WHO WE ARE

CloudSync Solutions Ltd. ("CloudSync", "we", "us") is a SaaS provider registered in Dublin, Ireland (Company No. IE-449821). We provide cloud-based file synchronisation and collaboration tools to businesses and individuals across the European Economic Area.

2. LAWFUL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases as defined by Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  (a) Consent — where you have given clear, informed, and unambiguous consent for us to process your data for a specific purpose, such as marketing communications.
  (b) Contractual necessity — where processing is required to deliver the services you have subscribed to under our Terms of Service.
  (c) Legal obligation — where we are required to process data to comply with applicable laws, including tax and anti-money-laundering regulations.
  (d) Legitimate interests — where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as fraud prevention and service improvement, provided these do not override your fundamental rights.

3. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect your name, email address, billing information, IP address, device identifiers, and usage logs. We do not collect special category data unless explicitly required and consented to.

4. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

  - Account data: retained for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months after account closure.
  - Billing records: retained for 7 years to comply with Irish tax law.
  - Usage logs: retained for 90 days, then anonymised for analytics.

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

5. YOUR RIGHTS AS A DATA SUBJECT

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  - Right of access (Article 15)
  - Right to rectification (Article 16)
  - Right to erasure / right to be forgotten (Article 17)
  - Right to restriction of processing (Article 18)
  - Right to data portability (Article 20)
  - Right to object to processing (Article 21)
  - Right not to be subject to automated decision-making (Article 22)

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@cloudsync-solutions.eu. We will respond within 30 days.

6. DATA SHARING

We share data only with the following categories of recipients, each bound by data processing agreements:

  - Amazon Web Services (EU-West-1) — infrastructure hosting
  - Stripe Payments Europe Ltd. — payment processing
  - Intercom R&D Unlimited — customer support tooling

We do not sell your personal data. International transfers outside the EEA are safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, and annual third-party penetration testing.
